About TNJA

“Judo”, A Martial art born out of jujitsu ,was founded by Dr. Jigaro kano in the year 1882 , with a perfect combination of displaying strength though spiritualism and science , philosophy through physics , making the art a perfect combination for a human to become a master of life.
Judo developed and evolved in a more philosophical way , preaching peace with a right to self defense in a “ Gentle Way” . To sum it up “MINIMUM EFFORT , MAXIMUM EFFICENCY” , is the motto of Judo , making it , a “WAY OF LIFE “.Swami Vivekananda’s saying “ A sound Mind in a sound body “ ,compliments Judo , as the art of JUDO , teaches management , enhances spiritual philosophical , physiological skills , builds character , strength ,patience and perseverance , with a strong mind to “ fall with grace and raise with resolve”, to the true practitioner.This famous Olympic sport , Simplifies fighting , teaching a physically inferior individual to overpower a superiorly built person technically and physiologically using laws of physics , like a crow bar moving huge boulders , which logically makes Judo the most apt , technically , scientifically proven self defense program for women and girls .
