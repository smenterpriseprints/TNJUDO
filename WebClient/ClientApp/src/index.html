About TNJA

TNJA has close to practitioners ,across the state , bringing several laurels to the state at national levels . TNJA has produced 32 black belts and 8 Sports Authority Of India certified coaches , who lay the path to propagate the art to newer heights within the next two years , with the help of the SDAT , government of Tamilnadu , the schools , colleges and the corporate world’s support .Judo , being a recognized Olympic Discipline , has enabled its practitioners to get student cash awards ranging between rs. 10000 to 200,000 through CM trophy, rds bds games , cbse , and khelo india games . The Students also are given a opportunity to get free quota seats in Medical, Engineering, Arts and Science collage’s . Judo also enables the player to get Central and state government Jobs as well as private Sector Jobs too.The manifest section of this portal shall testify the individual players enhancement through Judo in their life .
