About TNJA

TNJA has close to 5000 practitioners across the state, over the years, bringing several laurels to the state at national levels . TNJA has produced 32 black belts and 8 Sports Authority Of India certified coaches , who have laid the path to propagate the art to newer heights. Judo , being a recognized Olympic Discipline , has enabled its practitioners to get student scholarships and funds ranging between rs. 10000 to 200,000 through CM trophy, RDS BDS games , CBSE , and Khelo Indian games . The Students also are given an opportunity to get free quota seats in Medical, Engineering, Arts and Science college’s . Judo also enables the player to get central,state government and private sector jobs.The testimonial section of this portal shall testify the individual players enhancement through Judo in their life .
